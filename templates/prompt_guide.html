How to Write an Effective AI Prompt

Clear instructions help the AI produce better results. Keep these tips in mind when crafting your trading strategy prompts:

  • State your goal for the strategy in one or two sentences.
  • Provide any important constraints, such as risk limits or preferred indicators.
  • Mention how trades should be entered and exited.
  • Be explicit about time frames or assets if they matter.

Use short, direct sentences and specify only what is necessary for your backtest or paper session.
